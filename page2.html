Harmful effects of plastic on enviroments

Plastic pollution is currently one of the biggest enviroments concems. It may seem like large amount of plastic waste are inevitable in the world we live in but you can help with the plastic pollution issues by being of its dangers and taking steps to reduce waste

Following are some of the harmful effects of plastic on enviroments

  • Plastic spoils our groundwater
  • plastic attracts other pollution
  • plastic piles up in the enviroments
  • plastic threatens wildlife
  • Plastic poisons our food chain
